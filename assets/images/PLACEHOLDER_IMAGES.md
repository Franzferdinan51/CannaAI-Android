## Image Specifications

- **Format**: PNG for icons/images with transparency, JPG for photographs
- **Resolution**: High resolution (2x/3x for Retina displays)
- **Color Profile**: sRGB
- **Compression**: Optimized for mobile (balance quality vs file size)
- **Size**: Keep individual images under 500KB when possible

## Tools for Creating Images

- Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator
- Figma (for UI elements)
- Canva (quick mockups)
- GIMP (free alternative)
- Photos from your actual cannabis plants (for samples)

## Legal Considerations

- Ensure you have rights to all images used
- Stock photos may require proper licensing
- Plant photos should be taken in compliance with local laws
- Respect intellectual property rights
